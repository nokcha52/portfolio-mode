PCB type

  • Hotswap PCB

    Hotswap sockets allow you to easily swap switches. No soldering required — just pull out the old switch and push in a new one. For additional layout support you can consider a solder PCB.

    Solder PCB

    Requires soldering iron, solder, and desoldering tools for switch removal. Best for builders who prefer to solder their PCBs. For assembly without soldering you can consider a hotswap PCB.

What is the sound and feel difference between the POM plate and the Copper plate?

>> Mode Store repied:

Hi Sarah! Great question. The POM plate offers a softer, more flexible typing feel with a deeper, clackier sound profile. On the other hand, the Copper plate provides a much stiffer bottom-out feel and a denser, higher-pitched sound profile.
